# AI Tone Checklist

Use this checklist to diagnose Chinese scripts, posts, and article drafts.

## 1. Quotation Marks In Article Body

High-risk patterns:

- Chinese quotation marks around concepts, slogans, commands, or reader thoughts
- English quotation marks around concepts, slogans, commands, or reader thoughts
- Single quotation marks used for emphasis
- Quote-like corner brackets used only to create a polished writing effect

Why it feels artificial:

Frequent quotation marks make the article feel packaged and edited from above. They often turn normal speech into labels, slogans, or staged reader thoughts.

Rewrite direction:

Remove the quotation marks and fold the phrase into the sentence naturally.

```text
Before: 以前更多是在“给我建议”，现在开始能“替我动手”。
After: 以前更多是给我建议，现在开始能替我动手。
```

Marking rule:

When inspecting, mark any important sentence that uses quotation marks. In full rewrites, the revised article body should not contain quotation marks unless they are required by code, paths, URLs, or exact technical syntax.

## 2. Dashes Used As Rhetorical Punctuation

High-risk patterns:

- Em dashes used to create a polished pause
- En dashes used between title and explanation
- Double hyphens used like a dash
- Long horizontal bars used to make a sentence feel dramatic

Why it feels artificial:

Dashes often make Chinese articles sound like an edited presentation deck. They create a neat, staged rhythm instead of a spoken rhythm.

Rewrite direction:

Replace the dash with a comma, colon, period, line break, or a simpler sentence.

```text
Before: Browser — 写完页面自己打开验收
After: Browser，写完页面自己打开验收
```

Marking rule:

When inspecting, mark dashes used for rhetoric or formatting. In full rewrites, remove rhetorical dashes from the article body. Keep hyphens only inside exact names, paths, code identifiers, URLs, or terms where the hyphen is part of the correct spelling.

## 3. Speaking For The Reader

High-risk patterns:

- 很多人觉得...
- 你可能以为...
- 一提到...你脑子里可能会出现...
- 大部分人的第一反应是...
- 是不是...? 是不是...? 是不是...?

Why it feels artificial:

The writer enters the reader's mind, assigns them a thought, then corrects it. This can feel superior even when the argument is useful.

Rewrite direction:

State the author's observation directly.

```text
Before: 很多人觉得学 AI 最难的是信息太多跟不上。
After: 我这段时间越来越明显地感觉到：学 AI 不能只看二手解读。
```

## 4. Correction Structure

High-risk patterns:

- 不是 X，而是 Y
- 真正的问题不是 X，而是 Y
- 看起来是 X，本质上是 Y
- 你以为是 X，其实是 Y
- 真正重要的是...

Why it feels artificial:

The writer first builds a wrong answer, then announces the correct answer. Occasional contrast is fine; repeated contrast creates a lecturer posture.

Rewrite direction:

Say Y directly.

```text
Before: 真正卡住普通人的不是信息少，而是一直在看别人嚼过的内容。
After: 只看二手解读，很容易离原始信息越来越远。
```

## 5. Teacher-like Self Q&A

High-risk patterns:

- 那能不能...? 能。
- 要不要...? 当然要。
- 是不是很难? 其实不难。
- 有没有必要? 有，而且很重要。

Why it feels artificial:

It can sound like a teacher guiding a class toward the expected answer.

Rewrite direction:

Give the method, step, or judgment directly.

```text
Before: 那怎么找到一手信息源? 其实很简单。
After: 我现在主要从两个地方看一手 AI 信息：X 和 YouTube。
```

## 6. Lowering The Reader

High-risk terms:

- 普通人真正该...
- 小白一定要...
- 你只需要...
- 你肯定不知道...
- 很多人根本没意识到...

Why it feels artificial:

The reader may feel placed below the writer. This is especially obvious when identity labels repeat too often.

Rewrite direction:

Use experience anchors and optional paths.

```text
Before: 这才是普通人真正能开始的地方。
After: 如果现在不知道从哪里开始，可以先选一件每天重复做的小事。
```

## 7. Report-like Analysis Instead Of Lived Reaction

High-risk patterns:

- 这一步的关键是...
- 这个方法的核心是...
- 整体效果很好
- 这个感觉，我觉得对
- 改完之后变化很大

Why it feels artificial:

The writing analyzes from a distance instead of sounding like someone who experienced the thing.

Rewrite direction:

Use concrete, lived reaction, but do not fake emotion.

```text
Before: 这一步的关键是让它帮你提炼而不是总结。
After: 这一步我用下来最明显的感受是：让它做提炼，比让它做总结有用得多。
```

## 8. Generic Evaluation Without A Specific Target

High-risk patterns:

- 效果非常好
- 整体提升很大
- 优化效果很明显
- 几乎都被去掉了
- 改完之后变化很大

Why it feels artificial:

The writer gives a score but does not point the reader to a concrete place.

Rewrite direction:

Point to a specific sentence, section, moment, result, or visible change.

```text
Before: 效果非常好。
After: 第二段的说教感明显少了。
```

## What To Keep

Do not remove every direct "you". These are often fine:

- 如果你也在做这件事，可以先...
- 你可以对照自己的情况看...
- 这个方法适合...
- 具体可以这样做...

The key is: do not think for the reader, do not correct them from above, do not over-package phrases with quotation marks or dashes, and do not turn the draft into generic motivational writing.
